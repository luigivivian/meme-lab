## Decisions Made
- LegendWorker uses lazy renderer initialization to avoid importing FFmpeg-dependent modules when VIDEO_LEGEND_ENABLED=false
- Legend rendering runs AFTER the asyncio.gather for caption/hashtags/quality (sequential post-step) because it is I/O-heavy and depends on video being fully available on disk
- PostProductionLayer lazy-creates LegendWorker inside _safe_legend() to avoid circular imports and keep the dependency optional
