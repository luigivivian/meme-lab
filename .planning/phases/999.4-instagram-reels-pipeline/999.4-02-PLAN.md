**transcriber.py** — Gemini multimodal audio transcription to SRT.

- Provider from param or REELS_TRANSCRIPTION_PROVIDER (default "gemini")
- For "gemini" provider:
  - Use `_get_client()` from src.llm_client

  - Return output_path
