- run() delegates to per-step methods for DRY while maintaining full backward compatibility
- Segmentation greedy bin-packing: cenas accumulate until max_duration exceeded, then new segment starts
- Audio slicing uses ffmpeg -ss/-to with -c copy for speed; SRT slicing filters and re-indexes entries
